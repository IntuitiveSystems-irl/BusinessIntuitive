Business Intuitive Design System

The brand + UI system for Business Intuitive — the intelligence layer for founders. One dark oceanic base, a teal-lime signal layer, and a warm editorial paper surface. Fraunces for display, Inter for UI, JetBrains Mono for numbers.

Voice & Copy
Content principles
Direct. Proof-driven. No fluff. Numbers over adjectives.
✓ Write this
"Run the number."
"12 hours of admin reclaimed per week."
"Clarity is a system."
"The system your business should already have."
✕ Avoid this
"Empowering founders"
"Seamless business journey"
"Holistic growth platform"
"Next-generation solutions"
Casing rules
Sentence case — all headings and body copy
UPPERCASE — eyebrow labels and mono labels only, with 0.14em tracking
Title Case — product names and proper nouns (Business Intuitive, The Intuitive CEO, Intelligence)
No emoji in product or marketing copy. Use the lime dot only in pills.
