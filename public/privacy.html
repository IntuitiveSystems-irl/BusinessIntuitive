Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

Business Intuitive ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information — Name, phone number, email address, and business name
  • Business information — Company size, industry, website URL, and service area
  • Investor financing inputs — When you use the DSCR Quick Check calculator at /investor-financing, we receive the rent, principal & interest, taxes, insurance, HOA, and vacancy estimates you enter. We use these only to provide your DSCR result and, if you submit a lead form, to evaluate your scenario.
  • Communication records — Phone calls, SMS/text messages, and emails exchanged with our team
  • Website data — Pages visited, time on site, referrer, browser type, IP address, and approximate location (collected via standard analytics and advertising tags described in Section 4)
  • Publicly available information — Business listings, contractor license records, and publicly listed contact details

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Communicate with you about our services, including via phone calls and SMS/text messages
  • Provide and improve our diagnostic services and web systems
  • Manage our sales pipeline and customer relationships
  • Send appointment reminders, follow-ups, and service updates
  • Analyze and improve our business operations and website

3. SMS/Text Messaging Policy

Business Intuitive may send SMS/text messages to your phone number. By providing your phone number or initiating a text conversation with us, you consent to receive text messages from Business Intuitive related to:

  • Responses to your inquiries
  • Appointment scheduling and reminders
  • Service updates and follow-ups
  • Information about our products and services

How you consent to SMS

You may consent to receive text messages through one or more of the following methods:

  1. Inbound text — By sending a text message to our business number, you consent to receive replies and follow-up communications
  2. Website or form submission — By providing your phone number through our website or during a business inquiry with disclosure that SMS communications may follow
  3. Verbal consent — During phone calls, before any follow-up text messages are sent
  4. Business outreach — Business owners whose contact information is publicly listed may receive an initial outreach message with a clear opt-out option

How to opt out of SMS

You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. You may also contact us directly to request removal from our messaging list. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, pixels, and similar technologies on our websites to operate the site, analyze traffic, measure advertising performance, and improve user experience. The categories we use are:

  • Strictly necessary — Cookies required for the site and forms to function (e.g., session, security).
  • Analytics — Aggregated, non-identifying measurements of how the site is used (e.g., pages visited, time on page, traffic source).
  • Advertising / Conversion tracking — Cookies and tags from Google Ads (gtag.js, Google tag ID AW-18153637752) used to measure advertising performance, attribute conversions (such as form submissions, calls, and bookings), and, where you have opted in via Google's services, support remarketing.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may limit the functionality of the site (for example, lead forms or the DSCR calculator).

5. Advertising and Analytics Partners

We use the following third-party services to operate our advertising and analytics. Each service may collect information independently subject to its own privacy policy:

  • Google Ads / Google Tag (gtag.js) — Conversion tracking and audience measurement. Conversion ID AW-18153637752. See Google Privacy Policy. You can manage Google ad personalization at adssettings.google.com.
  • Calendly — Used for booking pre-approval and consultation calls. See Calendly Privacy Policy.
  • Resend — Used to deliver transactional emails (e.g., the Investor Financing Readiness Guide and lead notifications). See Resend Privacy Policy.
  • Twilio — Used for phone calls and SMS messages. See Twilio Privacy Policy.

Opting out of interest-based advertising

You can opt out of personalized Google ads at adssettings.google.com, or opt out of interest-based advertising across multiple ad networks at optout.aboutads.info and optout.networkadvertising.org. Most browsers also support a "Do Not Track" or "Global Privacy Control" signal that we honor where applicable.

6. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers — The third-party tools listed in Section 5 (Google, Calendly, Resend, Twilio), as well as cloud hosting and customer-relationship-management providers used to operate our business.
  • Lending partners — If you submit an investor financing scenario through the DSCR Quick Check or pre-approval booking flow at /investor-financing, we may share the contact and scenario details you provide with one or more vetted mortgage lenders so they can evaluate the deal and contact you about financing. We will only share information you have voluntarily provided for that purpose. You can decline at any time and request that we stop sharing your information by emailing us.
  • Sales representatives — Business Intuitive team members who manage your account or outreach.
  • Legal requirements — When required by law, regulation, or legal process.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information. Communication records are stored securely and access is limited to authorized team members.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including under the California Consumer Privacy Act, the Colorado Privacy Act, the Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act, and the EU/UK GDPR), you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Opt out of SMS messages by replying STOP
  • Opt out of personalized advertising as described in Section 5

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information in Section 10.
