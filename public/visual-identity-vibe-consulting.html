Your vibe is a system.

It is made of six pieces. When they're misaligned, you feel "off." When they line up, you feel powerful, grounded, and unmistakably yourself.

E
Energy

The frequency you radiate before you say a word. Soft, sharp, warm, electric — everyone reads it.

X
Expression

How you actually show up — voice, posture, the way you write, the way you dress, the way you build.

P

The visual signatures you return to over and over. Your color tendencies, textures, references, instincts.

T

The feeling-temperature of everything you put out. Calm, charged, gentle, decisive. This is what people remember.

Who you actually are — underneath the trends, the algorithms, and what you've been told to be.

The version of you that is becoming. The vibe has to leave room for her, and be built to grow into him.

FAQ

The actual questions.

Short answers to what most people ask before they book.

How do I send you my info?
After you book and pay, you will receive a short follow-up form to upload your 3–5 photos, share any links you want me to look at, and describe your current aesthetic. It takes about ten minutes.
Do I need to show my face?
No. You can stay anonymous if you prefer. Plenty of clients send only their visual world — brand shots, interior, product, mood — without a personal photo. The audit works either way.
How long does it take?
Three to five business days from when you send your intake materials. The deliverable includes a full written audit and a 10–15 minute Loom walkthrough.
Is this styling?
No. This is identity alignment. Styling is optional — clarity is the foundation. Once you know what your vibe actually is, you can style, build, hire, and post from a settled place. Without that, every outfit and every post is a guess.
