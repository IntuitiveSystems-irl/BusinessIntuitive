Section 3

Selection Criteria

Leads were sourced from Franklin County auditor parcel records, enriched with CoreLogic mortgage data, then skip-traced through BatchData / RealEstateAPI. The pipeline applied these filters:

  • 1
    County: Franklin County, OH (FIPS 39049). All 448,731 parcels ingested from county GIS.
  • 2
    Mortgage rate ≥ 8.00%: Filtered against CoreLogic mortgage records. Only parcels with a recorded interest rate at or above 8% survived.
  • 3
    Owner enrichment: Each parcel matched to an owner profile. Owner names normalized, LLC/trust entities resolved against Ohio Secretary of State filings.
  • 4
    Skip-trace: Emails and phone numbers appended via skip-trace providers. Address-match scoring at ≥ 0.6 confidence to avoid wrong-person contamination.
  • 5
    Compliance flags: Each contact tagged for DNC registry, litigator status, TCPA risk, and deceased status. Flagged leads are retained in the dataset but suppressed from phone outreach.
  • 6
    Deduplication: Same-person / same-property duplicates consolidated. Multi-property owners (e.g., Darryl Williams with 2 parcels) retained as separate rows for property-level targeting.
