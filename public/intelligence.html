FAQ

Common questions

Short, honest answers about what we build and how it gets delivered.

How are leads sourced?
County auditor parcel records, CoreLogic mortgage data, Secretary of State business filings, and enriched through skip-trace providers like BatchData and RealEstateAPI. Each record is matched, scored, and compliance-screened against DNC, TCPA litigator, and deceased flags before delivery.
What does the deliverable actually look like?
An enriched CSV of qualified leads with verified contact info, owner names, property addresses, mortgage details, compliance flags, and a 0–100 opportunity score per record. Accompanied by a written report covering market thesis, rate distribution, lead segmentation, sample profiles, scoring methodology, outreach recommendations, and illustrative lender economics.
How is this different from buying a generic list?
Generic lists are unscored, often stale, and ignore compliance. Market intelligence is scoped to your lending territory, filtered by the criteria that matter for your loan products, enriched with verified contact info, and screened for DNC, litigator, and deceased flags before it reaches you. The result is a working list, not a starting list.
How long does a Market Intelligence Brief take?
Typical turnaround is 3–7 business days per market, depending on county data availability, enrichment depth, and the scope of compliance screening required.
