What is a Strategic Systems Diagnostic?

A Strategic Systems Diagnostic is a structured assessment that maps a business's operational blind spots across four dimensions: strategy, systems, operations, and growth. It reveals where revenue leaks due to structural inefficiencies — not surface-level problems.

Unlike a standard business audit, the diagnostic focuses on the connections between systems. A business might have great marketing but a broken follow-up process. Great sales but no booking system. Great service but no referral engine. The diagnostic finds these gaps.

The 4 dimensions of a Strategic Systems Diagnostic

  1. Strategy alignment — Is your growth direction matched to your operational capacity?
  2. Systems architecture — Are your tools, platforms, and workflows connected or creating friction?
  3. Operational throughput — Are you converting inputs (leads, time, capital) into outputs (revenue, jobs, growth) efficiently?
  4. Growth bottlenecks — What is the single constraint preventing your next revenue tier?
